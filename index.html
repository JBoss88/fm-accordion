FAQS

What is Frontend Mentor and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers many free challenges to help you improve your front-end coding skills. They also have some premium challenges that require a paid subscription.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Including Frontend Mentor projects in your portfolio is an excellent way to showcase skills. Customizing them a little and adding your own personal touch can also be a nice way to show your creativity.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on challenge?

If you get stuck on a Frontend Mentor challenge, the best and quickest way to get help is to join their Discord server and ask questions in the dedicated "help" channel.
